Lasagna

A Hearty lasagna dish

Lasagna is a baked dish containing layers of boiled lasagna, and usually cheese, seasoned sauce of tomatoes, and meat or vegetables . It is possibly one of the oldest types of pasta

Lasagne is plural and refers to the noodles themselves, also plural. Lasagna is italian American parlance and refers to aforementioned chesy composition, the dish in toto
