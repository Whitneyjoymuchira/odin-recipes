Simple deviled eggs

Deviled eggs are a classic appetizer using boiled eggs, where the yolk is mixed with spices like Cayenne pepper to make them abit hotter and then spooned back into the egg whites

Deviled eggs are generally served cold as a side dish during gatherings or parties. THe dish's origin can been seen in recipes for boiled, seasoned eggs as far back as ancient Rome, where they traditionally served as a first course.
